PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project was developed as a student assignment for Jönköping University during the Web Design course in Spring Semester 2026.

It focuses on the design and development of an imaginary local business website that combines UI/UX principles, accessibility, and a clear visual identity.

The goal was to create a user-centered digital experience that reflects the café's concept of simplicity, nature, and a calm, mindful atmosphere.
