THE STORY BEHIND KOMOREBI

Komorebi started with a simple problem: It was difficult for us to find high-quality ceremonial matcha in cafes nearby.

This let us to Japan, where we experienced cafe culture in its purest form: calm spaces, rituals,and matcha served with care.

We wanted to bring that feeling back with us - creating a space where calmness, quality, and simplicity can be experienced.

OUR VALUES

SIMPLICITY

We believe in less - creating a calm space with simple ingredients and thoughtful design.

SUSTAINABILITY

We are commited to concious sourcing, reducing waste, and making choices that respect both people and the planet.

MINDFULNESS

Every drink is prepared with care, encouraging you to slow down and be present.

QUALITY & INGREDIENTS

We use high-quality ceremonial matcha, carefully selected for its smooth taste and vibrant color.

Our ingredients are kept simple and natural, focusing on purity and balance in every cup.

From plant-based milks to seasonal flavours, everything we serve is chosen to complement the matcha rather than overpower it - allowing its natural character to shine through.
